A gunman attacked a Planned Parenthood clinic in Boise, Idaho on March 28th, at approximately 1:00 PM. One doctor and two patients were killed and two nurses and three patients were injured. Once police arrived, the gunman turned the handgun on himself and fired. Paramedics pronounced him dead on scene. At 5:00 PM on March 28th he was identified as George Smith, of Emmet, Idaho which is around 30 miles away from Boise.

On March 30th, at 4:00 PM authorities released that they found a manifesto at George’s legal address. The full manifesto has not been released, but the Boise Police Department released the statement “The manifesto contained numerous references to the right to fetal life and the glory of martyrdom”.

The names of the deceased victims have not yet been officially released. However the husband and friends of Dr. Laura Bayer have stated that Laura was among the deceased on social media.
